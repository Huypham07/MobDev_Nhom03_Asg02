1. Overview
Online booking with online payment are available for Cine Complex Membership members, and only applied for select shows at Cine Complex. There is no fee to join Cine Complex Membership.

2. Refunds or Exchanges Policies
All tickets listed on the Cine Complex web site are General Admission - Adult unless otherwise noted.
Cine Complex Cinemas does not offer refunds or exchanges for successful tickets purchased on the Cine Complex Cinemas web site.
Refunds are allowed in the rare case when your account is billed but there is no matching order in our ticketing system. For any related matters, all you need to do is contact the cinema of your ticket purchase click here or you can email us at service@cj.net.
The standard refund processing times are as follows:
1. ATM Card (Vietnam Domestic) transactions will be refunded within 1 working week.
2. VISA/ MasterCard (Vietnam Domestic) will be refunded within 1 working month.
3. ATM Vietcombank cardholders will be refunded within 48 working hours.

Please review your tickets and personal information carefully before your payment is processed.

3. Confirmation SMS & letter
Upon completing your ticket purchase or reservation, you will receive a booking confirmation to your email address and FREE SMS confirmation to your mobile number as indicated on your order.
This email and SMS are a backup confirmation to your actions on the order screen. Please carefully choose the cinema location, movie title, session time, and seats before completing your order.
Your confirmation email may go to your junk mail box, so please check that before contacting the cinema. If you have any questions or problems with your order, you can contact us at: service@cj.net for support.
